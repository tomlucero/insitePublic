CSU Bookstore Shipping Policy

When your order is placed before Noon on Monday-Friday, your order will ship or be ready for pick-up the same day on in-stock items! Orders placed after Noon on Monday-Friday will be processed the next business day.

Plan Ahead! Due to the high volume of textbook orders received during the final two weeks of January and August, please allow an additional business day before your order ships or is ready for pickup.

International Shipments

All Orders Shipping rates start at $39.99
  • International shipment rates start at $39.99. International Shipment rates vary depending on your items' destination, carrier, weight, and dimensions. 
  • Handling fees on oversized items still apply to International shipments. 
  • Before shipping your order, the CSU Bookstore will email you shipping quotes with shipping rates and estimated transit times. Please respond to this email so we can promptly send your shipment to you.
  • Please allow an additional 24 hours for the CSU Bookstore to process your International Order. 
  • All international orders are subject to approval by the Colorado State University Export Control department. Some items can not be shipped internationally. We will notify you of any restrictions before completing your order.
  • International Shipping charges are non-refundable. 
  • The customer is responsible for all duties, taxes, tariffs, fees, and other charges assessed by customs agencies. The CSU Bookstore does not declare purchases as "gifts" to avoid duties/taxes. 
  • Due to university export control regulations, the CSU Bookstore cannot ship to freight forwarders. Orders shipping to freight forwarders will be held until we get destination shipment information, and may be cancelled if destination shipment information is not provided.  
     

Special Handling Fees

Some items we carry require special handling from our carrier. These items are usually very fragile, bulky, or heavy. Items with a handling fee include: 

  • Diploma Frames 
  • Canopy Tents 
  • Sculptures 
  • Furniture

Any additional handling fees will be disclosed on the item page. Handling charges are only charged on orders for shipment; orders for in-store pickup are not assessed a handling fee. Handling fees are non-refundable. 

Back Order Shipments

Customers are not charged for additional shipments to fulfill back-ordered merchandise and textbook orders.

In-Store Pick Up at the Lory Student Center

For your convenience, you can order online and pick up your order at the CSU Bookstore! Our in-store pick up window in the Lory Student Center makes it convenient to pick up your textbooks, supplies, or CSU Rams gear!

Orders for In-Store Pick Up are held for 14 days from the day we notify you the order is ready to pick up. Orders left longer than 14 days are restocked, and may be charged a restocking fee.
